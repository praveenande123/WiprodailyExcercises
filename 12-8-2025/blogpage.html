The Beauty and Basics of Badminton

By Praveen Kumar Ande | August 12, 2025

Badminton is a fast-paced and exciting sport enjoyed by millions. Whether for fun or competition, it keeps you active and builds friendships.

Getting Started

All you need is a racket, shuttlecock, and net. Play indoors or outdoors. Hit the shuttle over the net and land it in your opponent's court.

Benefits

Badminton improves hand-eye coordination, agility, and fitness. It's a great way to relieve stress and have fun.

Tips for Beginners

Start with basic shots and footwork. Practice often. Enjoy the game and stay consistent!
